Privacy Policy

MeasureX values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when you use the MeasureX application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Automatically Collected Information

The application may collect limited information automatically to ensure proper operation and improve stability, including:

  • Device model and operating system version
  • Application version and usage statistics
  • Crash logs and diagnostic data

This data is collected through Firebase and Firebase Crashlytics and is used solely for app performance monitoring and crash analysis.

1.2 Location Information

The Compass feature may request location permission in order to determine and display your current geographic location. Location data is:

  • Used only while the Compass feature is active
  • Not stored permanently
  • Not shared with third parties

1.3 Microphone Access

The Noise Detection feature requires microphone access to measure ambient sound levels. Audio data is:

  • Processed locally on your device
  • Not recorded, stored, or transmitted

2. How We Use Information

Collected information is used exclusively to:

  • Provide and maintain core functionality
  • Improve app stability and performance
  • Diagnose and fix crashes

3. Data Sharing

MeasureX does not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties. Data collected by Firebase is handled in accordance with Google’s privacy policies.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable technical measures to protect collected information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

5. Children’s Privacy

MeasureX is not intended for children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
